Privacy Policy for QR Quest

QR Quest is a simple and lightweight Android application developed by Berreix solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use QR Quest.

Information Collection and Use

QR Quest does not collect or store any personal information from its users. The app solely accesses the device camera for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Camera Permission

QR Quest requires access to your device's camera in order to function properly. This permission is solely used for the scanning of QR codes and barcodes within the app. We do not access, collect, or store any images or videos captured by your device's camera.

Use of Scan Results

The scan results obtained by QR Quest are displayed within the app for the convenience of the user. The app provides options to copy the scan result to the clipboard or open it in the device's web browser. QR Quest does not track or store any information related to the scan results.

Third-Party Services

QR Quest does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs. The app operates entirely on the user's device and does not transmit any data externally.

Children's Privacy

QR Quest is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

By using QR Quest, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.
