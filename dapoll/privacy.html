Privacy Policy for DaPoll

Effective Date: Oct 16, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to DaPoll. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using our app.

Information We Collect

When you use DaPoll, we collect the following information:

  • Email: Used for account creation and authentication.
  • Username: Required for account creation and identifying users.
  • Profile Photo: Optional, provided by the user during account setup.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial or health information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation: Your email, username, and profile photo are necessary for setting up and maintaining your account.
  • App Functionality: To ensure a seamless user experience.
  • Sending OTP Emails: We use Brevo to send OTP emails during account creation and login.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We use Brevo to send OTP emails, but we are not certain whether Brevo stores your email address. We recommend reviewing Brevo’s privacy policy for more information on their data practices.

We do not share your data with any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Retention

Your data is securely stored on our cloud servers. We retain your data as long as you maintain an account with us. Once you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers.

User Rights

  • Data Access and Update: You can view your username and profile photo in your account settings. However, once your username and profile photo are set during account creation, they cannot be changed.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data through the “Delete Profile” option available in the app’s profile screen.

Security of Your Information

We prioritize the security of your data. We use HTTPS to secure communication between our app and servers. Please note that while our app is passwordless, we do not currently encrypt user-provided data.

Children's Privacy

DaPoll is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Location Data

DaPoll does not collect, track, or store any location data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your privacy rights, please contact us at support@berreix.com.

By using DaPoll, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.
