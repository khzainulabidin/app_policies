Data Deletion Process

At DaPoll, we provide users with the ability to delete their account and all associated data. Below is an outline of the process:

How to Delete Your Profile:

  • Open the DaPoll application.
  • Navigate to the Profile screen.
  • Tap the Delete Profile button.

Once you delete your profile, your account and all personal data (email, username, profile photo) will be deleted immediately from our servers.

Important Note on Polls

While your account and personal information will be permanently deleted, any polls where you have previously voted will retain your vote count. This ensures the integrity of the polling data, but no personal information related to your vote will be accessible or stored.

If you have any questions or issues related to account deletion, please contact us at support@berreix.com.
